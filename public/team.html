Adam’s leadership and vision have provided the world’s leading companies with insightful consultation broadening perspectives about business and competitive challenges influencing executive selection, the foundation for Adam’s track record of continuously exceeding client expectations.

Emerging entities, growth companies, and Fortune 500 corporations have sought Adam to build the right leadership team for their specific situation, talent that meets both the challenges of a global economy and those inherent in corporate maturation. Adam leads a collaborative group of professionals in establishing new standards of service, based on the time-tested best practices he developed for conduct of the entire search process. Timely execution, presentation of original candidates, and successful recruitment of the unexpected individual are hallmarks of delivery.

Psychology is the basis for his ability to create outstanding and unexpected solutions for clients, establishing his reputation for identifying and attracting candidates with the ability to meet immediate and future challenges. Clients find Adam is a significant factor in a placements’ transition and smooth assimilation in the organization.

Recognized for his leadership and governance expertise, Adam’s opinions frequently appear in major business and industry publications. He is based in the firm’s Cleveland office.

Benjamin Stocum - Operating Partner

Benjamin Stocum is the President of Delta Diversified, Inc. and Founding Partner of Delta Partners. As the Operating Partner for the firm, Benjamin and his team ensure that all of our personnel and resources are being accurately deployed with a sole focus on exceeding the expectations for each and every client engagement. Our commitment to Performance, Quality & Excellence requires seamless collaboration and coordination of: research and business intelligence, data & analytics, marketing & communications, rigorous search process and intense focus on the critical-to-quality deliverables that are coveted by our clients.

Since beginning his executive search career in 2004, Benjamin has been dedicated to client service, providing some of the world’s most prestigious corporations with senior executive level and succession planning human capital solutions. He has placed Chief Officers, Vice Presidents and Sr. Director level executives across corporate functions, including: Accounting & Finance, Engineering, Operations/General Management, Sales & Marketing, Human Resources, Legal, Risk & Compliance and Professional Services.

Prior to his leadership role with Delta Partners and Delta Diversified, Benjamin was a Director in the Global Professional Services Practice of executive search firm, CTPartners. During his tenure, he executed searches in the United States, Canada, Germany, Italy, Japan and Guam.

A graduate of Heidelberg College, Benjamin and his family reside in Mayfield, OH and he is based in the Cleveland, OH office.
