Delta Partners, the retained executive search business of Delta Diversified,Inc., today announced that industry veteran Adam P. Kohn is joining the firm as Managing Partner. Adam will be leading strategic growth and business development efforts for the firm out of their Cleveland, OH office.

Adam is bringing Delta Partners over 25 years of experience, which includes over 500 successful C-suite and senior executive placements across industry verticals, such as: Industrial Manufacturing, Engineering, Construction & Environmental Services, Professional Services and Technology. As the only vertically integrated, turnkey search firm in the U.S., Delta Partners and our affiliate companies have the flexibility to help our clients connect the “talent dots” between high-level executive strategy to hands-on execution quickly, efficiently and with the highest-caliber candidates.

Prior to his most recent leadership role, as Senior Partner with KingsleyGate Partners, Adam held senior client service and global practice leadership roles as a Partner & Vice Chairman with industry pioneers Christian & Timbers and CTPartners.

“It’s all about the clients and Adam knows that as well as anyone in the world of executive search.” says Benjamin Stocum, Founding & Operating Partner of Delta Partners. “With Adam joining our team, we are able to more comprehensively meet the strategic executive leadership needs of our clients, which is really what it’s all about. In addition, Adam’s proven philosophies and methodologies on the search and placement profession will positively impact each and every member of our team. We are all genuinely excited about this addition.”
